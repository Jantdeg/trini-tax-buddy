Executive Summary

The Budget 2026 introduces several NEW taxation measures while maintaining most existing personal income tax deductions and rates. The major changes focus on new revenue streams, business taxation, and National Insurance System reforms rather than direct personal income tax adjustments.

17. IMPORTANT EFFECTIVE DATES

CONCLUSION

Budget 2026 represents a significant shift in Trinidad and Tobago's tax policy, focusing on:

  1. Broadening the tax base through new revenue streams (landlords, banks, luxury goods)

  2. Protecting household consumers (gas price reduction, small LPG cylinders unchanged, residential electricity exempt)

  3. Ensuring NIS sustainability through necessary but gradual reforms

  4. Supporting key sectors (agriculture, education, healthcare)

  5. Maintaining core personal tax structure (rates and major deductions unchanged)

The budget emphasizes "economic fairness" by targeting those most able to pay (banks, luxury car buyers, high-earning landlords) while protecting ordinary citizens and even reducing fuel costs.
